Our Mission

What We Do

A community-led research initiative turning ambition into output.

The Green Compass started as a research group with some members. Now we are opening our doors to a wider community of people who care about environment, sustainability and the future.

We believe in action over attendance. We don't force participation. But if you raise your hand for a task, we expect you to complete it. Our goal is to produce real research outputs — papers, reports, policy briefs — while helping members build career-relevant skills.
